About Altech Africa Limited

Altech Africa was founded in 2006, its head office being in Nairobi Kenya, has pursued a clear goal to fill an industry need for an expert firm covering a very broad range of services and capabilities. It has been operating under business name Altech Africa until its incorporation on June 23rd. 2009 and now operates as Altech Africa Limited.

Systems integration, consultancy, outsourcing and project management services.

Altech Africa Ltd specializes in systems integration, consultancy, outsourcing and project management services. Our total solutions are backed up with the ability to manage projects from conception, implementation and Commissioning.customers and partners deliver products and services to their customers, we pride ourselves in our ability to integrate diverse technology skills in the delivery of comprehensive business solutions.

At Altech Africa Ltd, we balance our technical talent with practical business sense and real dedication to customer satisfaction. We understand that the power of technology can be harnessed most effectively when tempered with the ability to plan and implement reliable, supportable solutions.

Equipment installation and commissioning

Automation using Network Operating Systems
